## Email to New Internal Testers

**Subject: You're in! Here's how to join the Nagz beta on TestFlight**

Thanks for volunteering to test Nagz! I'm adding you as an internal tester. Here's what will happen and what you need to do:

### What I'm doing on my end

I'm adding you to App Store Connect with the **Marketing** role. This gives you access to TestFlight builds without any app management permissions. You'll need to accept an invitation from Apple before you can install the app.

### Step 1: Accept the App Store Connect invitation

You'll receive an email from Apple (no_reply@email.apple.com) with the subject **"You're invited to join a development team on App Store Connect."**

- Tap the link in the email
- Sign in with your Apple ID (the same email I added you with)
- Accept the invitation

If you don't already have an Apple ID associated with this email, you'll be prompted to create one. Check spam if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

### Step 2: Install TestFlight

If you don't already have it, download Apple's TestFlight app from the App Store:
https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664

### Step 3: Get the Nagz beta

Once you've accepted the App Store Connect invitation, you'll receive a second email from Apple inviting you to test Nagz on TestFlight. Tap **"View in TestFlight"** to accept, then open the TestFlight app and tap **Install** next to Nagz.

If you don't get the second email, open TestFlight — the app may already appear there.

### Step 4: Create your Nagz account

Open Nagz and tap **"Sign Up."** You'll need:
- An email address
- A password
- A display name

Then either **create a family** (you'll get an invite code to share) or ask another tester for their invite code to **join an existing family**.

### Things to try

- **Chat tab** — type "Remind my spouse to call me at lunchtime tomorrow" or "What's overdue?"
- **Nag someone** — create a reminder for another family member and watch the notifications
- **Per-nag chat** — open any reminder, tap the chat icon, and negotiate with the AI
- **Siri** — say "Remind me in Nagz" for a quick self-reminder
- **Connections** — invite someone outside your family via the People tab
- **Shake to report** — shake your phone anytime to send feedback through TestFlight

**Note:** AI Chat requires iPhone 15 Pro or newer (it uses Apple's on-device AI). On older phones, all other features work — you just won't see the Chat tab.
