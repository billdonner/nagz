## Email to Testers

**Subject: Welcome to the Nagz beta — here's how to get started**

Thanks for signing up to test Nagz! Here's how to get the app on your phone:

**Step 1: Install TestFlight**
If you don't already have it, download Apple's TestFlight app from the App Store:
https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664

**Step 2: Accept the invite**
I've added your email to our TestFlight group in App Store Connect. You should receive an email from Apple with the subject "You're invited to test Nagz." Tap "View in TestFlight" to accept.

If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. It comes from no_reply@email.apple.com.

**Step 3: Install Nagz**
Open the TestFlight app → tap "Install" next to Nagz. It installs like any other app.

**Step 4: Create your account**
Open Nagz and tap "Sign Up." You'll need an email, password, and display name. Then create a family or ask another tester for their invite code to join theirs.

**Tips for testing:**
- **AI Chat** (Chat tab) requires iPhone 15 Pro or newer — it runs entirely on your device
- Try saying things like "Remind me to take out the trash tomorrow" or "What's overdue?"
- Try Siri: "Remind me in Nagz"
- Shake your phone to send feedback directly through TestFlight

Full test plan: https://nagz.online/index.html

Questions? Just reply to this email. Happy nagging!
